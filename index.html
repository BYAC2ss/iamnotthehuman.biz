What is Bunny girl senpai about?

Bunny Girl Senpai is a heartwarming romantic comedy anime that explores the complexities of adolescence, emotions, and relationships. Follow the story of Sakurajima Mai and the struggles of growing up while balancing personal feelings and social expectations.
